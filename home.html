ARTIST PROFILE

Stage Name

DJ Mthizanation (MTHIZA NATION)

Real Name

Thembelani Sikhona Buthelezi

Date of Birth

29 June 2000

Origin

Empangeni / Endlangubo, KwaZulu-Natal

Born and raised in the rural heartlands of Empangeni and Endlangubo in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa, DJ Mthizanation is one of the most exciting upcoming DJs and producers of the new generation. Known for his electrifying stage presence and energetic sound, he blends Amapiano, Afro House, and modern electronic rhythms to create unforgettable musical experiences.

Inspired by Black Coffee, Black Motion, DJ Tira, DJ Maphorisa, Enoo Napa, and DJ Oskido, Mthizanation blends Afro House, Amapiano, and electronic African rhythms into a powerful high-energy experience.

His performances continue to attract attention across KwaZulu-Natal and beyond, positioning him as one of South Africa’s most exciting upcoming DJs.
